Shadow Dash Project

A pixel mobile game

13-Dec-2019

The Shadow Dash Project is my first proper try at making a game. It will have very simple mechanics and easy controls. I am putting the difficulty bar at the limits of what I know so it will be as much of a learning experience as it can possibly be while still becoming a proper product.

Let's start by answering the most important question; What do you do in the game? Well, for starters. It will be a fighting arena type of game. The playable character, will fight in different looking arenas that will essentially be one arena with multiple looks. Below is a picture of the first concept. A bamboo forest arena.
